Career Level

An important step in your career development process is understanding how to read your current career level and map it to the ideal complexity level for a job in the future. You can save this customized report in your Career Vault and share parts of this with hiring managers.

Career level is an empirical score arrived at after analyzing answers to a series of questions related to the complexity of a role, across various factors. It is indicative of the distance travelled so far in a career, assuming that higher level of experience entails handling greater levels of complex roles. Thus, higher complexity in a role makes for a higher career level.

Understanding this is vital to your career development. With this, you can focus on securing fulfilling roles with greater levels of complexity in the future and not just on salary or an organization with a 'name' or �employer-brand'.
