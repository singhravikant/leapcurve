Your career level growth score tells you where you stand against other companies, industries and functions in terms of career level growth. It takes into account factors like ease of getting promoted and highest career level reachable.

Your salary growth score tells you where you stand against other companies, industries and functions in terms of salary growth. It takes into account factors like salary growth at the highest and next level and average salary growth from one career level to the next.

In most industries the number of roles decreases with higher Career Levels - creating "red zones", i.e. zones that have barriers to growth, promotions & role availability.

In case you find yourself at these red zones, you may experience promotion barriers, unavailability of roles within your organization or outside and high time-to-offer from companies you may be interviewing with.
